🔒 Privacy Policy

1. General Provisions

SolveMap (hereinafter referred to as "we", "our", "the Site") respects your privacy. This policy describes how we handle information when you use our website solvemap.space and related services.

2. What Data We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data of our users. Our website is designed for free access to educational materials (PDF guides, videos, pins).

🔐 Important: You are not required to register, create an account, or provide any personal information to use our site.

3. What We Do Not Collect

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers
  • IP addresses (except for standard analytics)
  • Browsing history or behavioral data
  • Payment or financial information
  • Tracking cookies (only technical cookies are used)

4. Automatic Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze website traffic. This is a standard service that collects anonymised statistics: visitor count, pages visited, traffic sources.

This data cannot be used to identify any specific individual and is used solely to improve content quality.

5. Third-Party Services

  • YouTube, TikTok, Pinterest: We use their APIs to automatically publish educational content. These platforms have their own privacy policies.
  • Google Analytics: Collects anonymised traffic statistics.
  • exe.io: Used to shorten links to PDF files.

6. Use of Cookies

We use a minimal set of technical cookies required for the website to function properly. We do not use advertising, tracking, or third-party cookies.

7. Links to Third-Party Resources

Our site may contain links to other websites (Pinterest, YouTube, exe.io, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy policies of these resources. We recommend reviewing their policies before using them.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us via Telegram: @solvemap or by email: solvemap.space@gmail.com

9. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy. All changes will be posted on this page. The current version is dated June 20, 2026.
